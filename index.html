CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

Welcome to cystal collector! You will be given a random number every round. Your goal is to get your total score to match the random number to win the round. If you exceed the random number, you lose!

There are 4 different crystals, each with a different value that will be randomly assigned to them at the start of the round. Select the appropriate crystals to add your total score to the random numner
